Reflection

Lesson 1: Feedback

Writing is like music: It makes no sense to play so that no one can hear. The only time you should ever write for no one else to see it is if you keeping a journal for the purposes of seeing personal progress. Barring that, it makes ZERO sense to keep your work hidden.

Benefits of Sharing your work:

  • You'll have the opportunity to make someone smile
  • You may recieve helpful tips to improve your work
  • It could get picked up by a literary agent (theoretically at least)
  • Regularly sharing your work can provide greater motivation to practice writing

Benefits of NOT sharing your work:

  • There aren't any
  • You don't have to do any editing (c'mon, that's a lame reason!)
  • You can't make anyone's day - BOOO!
  • Are you still reading? Share your work!
